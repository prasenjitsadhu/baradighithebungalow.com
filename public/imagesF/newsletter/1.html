Every Visit is a Story

When the British landed on India’s shores, they knew little of the vast expanse of land they would soon claim and use to produce the Chinese drink they would soon swear by. As it were, the-soon-to-be fantastic discovery of tea would give work to generations, change the landscape of forests in areas like North Bengal forever, and create a titanic industry, and give birth to heritage tea bungalows… All while leaving behind the scenic tea gardens – abodes for seekers of tranquil, and saviours from the hustle of the future metropolis.

Amenities & Experiences

At Baradighi – The Bungalow, experience the little pleasures of life amidst the enrapturing nature. Get a taste of the beautiful scenery as you stroll amidst the tea gardens, or go camping by the river side under a starry blanket of nightfall.

Kitchen Garden

Most of the food served at Baradighi is grown, organically in our Kitchen Garden. Plant a tree in your name, or pick your choice for a scrumptious meal.

Guide & Library

Timeless books are an ideal retreat for hours of tales in the middle of my best solitary moments, and a personal guide at all times, for secretes beyond words.

Riverside Activities

A riverside camping arrangement can be set up, with tents for 6 people on a wooden deck or open tents or deck chairs, facing the river. Rafting may also be arranged.

Banquet Hall

Accommodating 30 people, our modern boardroom is ideal for those willing to combine business with leisure or for a homely reception party.

Wi-Fi & Connectivity

Experience the recluse of Dooars with the connectivity of an Urban City, enjoy all modern amenities with a heritage charm at Baradighi - The Bungalow.

Sports Facilities

A well-equipped sports complex within the bungalow premises that allows you a day of Table Tennis, Badminton, Carom, Chess & Archery.
